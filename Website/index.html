WHAT PROBLEM ARE WE SOLVING?

Take back control of your mornings...

We've all been there, dragging yourself out of bed is hard enough without having to come out to a car thats fogged up, adding yet more time to your journey.

That's where FogMeister comes in.

HOW IT WORKS

Get a notification when the FogMeister device senses your car windscreen has fogged up

Use our cloud based system to notify the FogMeister device to start its dehumidifier to demist your car when you desire.

Enjoy getting in your car to a clear windscreen with no need to wait around for it to demist.

PRICING

SENSOR

Allows automated and continual humidity detection

SOFTWARE

Provides the user interface to detect humidity and allow control of the humidifier

DEHUMIDIFIER

Remove the humidity in the car to a level that makes the fog on your windscreen disappear
