WHAT PROBLEM ARE WE SOLVING?

Take back control of your mornings...

We've all been there, dragging yourself out of bed is hard enought without having to come out to a car thats fogged up, adding yet more time to your journey.

That's where FogMeister comes in.

How it Works

FogMeister will continuously monitor the humidity in your car in order to detect wehn the windscreen has fogged up. When it detects this, a notification will be sent to your phone. You then have the option to activate a dehumidifier in the car such that by the time you need to get in, your car will be ready to drive and you can avoid any further delays to your journey!
